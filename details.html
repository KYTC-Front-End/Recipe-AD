Banana Pancakes

These banana pancakes are a delicious, easy weekend breakfast. They're fluffy, moist, and filled with spiced banana flavor Pass the maple syrup! Traditional pancakes are made with refined flour and sugar, then topped with more sugar in the form of syrup. This creates sugar and carb-heavy meal that creates blood sugar fluctuations that aren't great for health in the short term or long term .

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Directions

step 1

Mash the banana. Peel the banana and break it up into several big chunks in a medium bowl

step 2

Add flavorings if desired then Stir in the eggs.

step 3

Heat a pan over medium heat.

step 4

Cook for about 1 minute.

step 5

Sprinkle with toppings , then Flip the pancakes.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
